RESEARCH

　

Research topics:

　

Action and Gesture Recognition

Texture based methods are investigated for describing actions and gestures. Static texture operators with temporal template and dynamic texture operators are utilized to represent motion.
Speaker recognition by mouth movements

To date much research has focused on the acoustic feature for speaker recognition, relatively little work has been done in investigating the visual features. Dynamic LBP texture and structural EdgeMap features were combined to take both motion and appearance together into account, providing the description ability for spatiotemporal development in speech.
Visual speech recognition & synthesis

Visual speech information plays an important role in speech recognition under noisy conditions or for listeners with hearing impairment. Local spatiotemporal descriptors were proposed  to represent and recognize spoken isolated phrases based solely on visual input.

　

 Facial expression recognition

A goal of this research is to determine the emotional state from facial expressions using dynamic information analysis. Other modalities will also be used to supplement facial expressions for reliable emotion recognition.
Dynamic texture analysis

Dynamic texture (DT) is an extension of texture to the temporal domain. Description,  recognition, segmentation and synthesis of dynamic textures has attracted growing attention. New methods: VLBP and LBP-TOP for recognizing dynamic textures are proposed. DT segmentation and synthesis are investigated.

　

 Gait recognition

Gait is an identifying biometric feature. In recent years, Video-based gait recognition is becoming a new challenging problem in the field of computer vision. 2D and 3D descriptors were proposed for gait recognition.

　

Tracking and human motion analysis

Tracking, motion recognition and simulation are important in human motion analysis. We implemented effective tracking and the experimental motion analysis system based on retrieval.

　

 Digital human

Digital human has been a highlight in both life sciences and information sciences. Digital human integrates the achievement of medicine and computer science up to the minute, attempt to establish the computer model with all hierarchies.
Color transfer

Color transfer between images or from image to video making efficient use of the image elementary statistical information, is a fresh method altering the image or video color. We proposed   the higher moments: skewness and kurtosis for efficient   color transfer.

Stereoscopic Images

In the virtual reality technology, the vision display system must settle the problem of stereoscopic display and stereoscopic image resource to build up the immersion sense of VR. We proposed three methods of generating stereoscopic image: moving flatly, cylindrical transform and spherical transform for generating stereoscopic images.
